The Young Researchers‚Äô Symposium (YRS) at SPELL 2025 invites submissions from motivated undergraduate, postgraduate, and PhD students, as well as early-career researchers from academia or research-intensive industry roles. We invite submissions from:

Submission Format and Guidelines

We are accepting short papers that describe original, unpublished work. A short paper is not simply a compressed long paper. Instead, it should present a concise, focused contribution.

Submission involves: One-page short paper for review, and upon acceptance, preparation of a poster along with a 15-minute presentation ‚Äì to be submitted for camera-ready submission.

Typical short paper contributions include:
  • Novel research insights or application nuggets
  • (Non-replicated) Reproducibility studies (positive or negative)
  • Limitations or methodological reflections
  • Conceptual or opinion-driven cases
